Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your MD Collection purchase. If for any reason you are not happy, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Item must be in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, please contact us via WhatsApp or our contact form. Exchanges are subject to stock availability. Delivery charges for exchanges are covered by the customer unless the item received was faulty.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed via M-Pesa or Original Payment Method within 48 hours.
